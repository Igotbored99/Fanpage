THE ELDER SCOLLS V: Dragonborn

The Elder Scrolls V: Dragonborn is the third and final add-on for Skyrim. Embark on a journey to the island Solstheim and uncover the mystery surrounding the Dragonborn of the past and the Daedric Prince of forbiddin knowledge, Hermaeous Mora. The Dovahkiin must save the world once again.


After the threats of Alduin, The World Eater and Lord Harkon, The Vampire Lord. A new threat to the world emerges in the form of another Dragonborn, The very first. Fate has decided that the first and last Dragonborns must battle to decide on the fate of the world.
